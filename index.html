Too much sleeping cash in bank accounts

Far too many people have their money sitting in a bank account with 0 percent interest. According to the danish central bank and politiken the average bank account balance is now 185.000 DKK.
While some of this may be attributable to risk preferences, it is definitely also in large part due to lacking time and knowledge.
This is why we want to give people an opportunity to invest with an intuitive and flexible app. No meetings at the bank required. No need to understand terms like "ÅOP" - just download the app, type in your information, and put your money to work!

Investment

Drawing on insight from cognitive psychology and behavioral economics we know that the key to making more people invest is to make it simple, safe and flexible.
In order to keep things simple we have made the bold choice to offer only one investment option: The danish C20 index.
A ton of investment platforms already offer choice in excess. On the contrary the C20 index offers the possibility of high returns without excessive risk, and by allowing for only one choice, we give users the opportunity to get a return on their money without having to engage in speculation about the future development of particular stocks.
